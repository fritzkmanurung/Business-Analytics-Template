Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new report?

Navigate to the Reports page and click the "Generate Report" button. Select your desired date range, filters, and export format, then click "Generate".

How do I export data?

You can export data in CSV or Excel format from the Reports page. Apply your desired filters, then click one of the export buttons at the top of the page.

Can I customize the dashboard widgets?

Yes! Go to Settings > Dashboard Preferences to customize which widgets appear on your dashboard and their order.

How do I manage API keys?

Go to Settings > API Keys to generate, view, copy, or revoke your API keys. Make sure to store them securely as they cannot be retrieved after creation.

What browsers are supported?

We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox.
